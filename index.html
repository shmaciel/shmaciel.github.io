I'm an undergraduate student at the University of São Paulo (USP), in Brazil, majoring in mathematics. This semester I'm at the Australian National University in an exchange program.

My mathematical interests extend to various fields but mostly algebraic topology, category theory and related areas.
In particular, I'm highly interested in abstract homotopy theory, higher category theory, K-theory and higher algebra.
I'm part of the organization of a student-run group of seminars, the S4 Group, that takes place at the University of São Paulo itself.
